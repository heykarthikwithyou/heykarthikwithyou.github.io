Features

  • 12.7-Inch 3K Display – Large high-resolution screen with sharp visuals ideal for movies, gaming, reading, and productivity.
  • 144Hz Refresh Rate – Ultra-smooth scrolling and responsive touch experience for gaming and multitasking.
  • MediaTek Dimensity 8300 Processor – Powerful chipset designed for gaming, AI features, and efficient multitasking.
  • 12GB RAM + 256GB Storage – Excellent memory and storage combination for apps, media, and professional workflows.
  • Pen Plus Stylus Support – Suitable for note-taking, sketching, studying, and creative tasks.
  • AI-Enabled Features – Smart productivity and optimization features for improved user experience.
  • Quad JBL Speakers – Rich and immersive audio performance for OTT streaming and gaming.
  • Massive 10200mAh Battery – Long-lasting battery backup for all-day usage.
  • 45W Fast Charging – Faster charging support for reduced downtime.
  • WiFi 6E Connectivity – Faster and more stable wireless internet performance.
  • Premium Slim Design – Modern lightweight build suitable for portability.
  • Android Productivity Experience – Supports multitasking, split-screen usage, and entertainment apps.

Pros

  • Stunning 3K display quality with smooth 144Hz refresh rate
  • Powerful performance for gaming and multitasking
  • Excellent speaker quality with JBL tuning
  • Large battery backup supports long usage sessions
  • Stylus support improves productivity and creativity
  • Premium design feels modern and high-end
  • Strong value compared to premium flagship tablets
  • Large screen works great for OTT streaming and study use

Cons

  • Android tablet app optimization still varies for some apps
  • Large tablet size may feel less portable for some users
  • Cameras are functional but not flagship-level
  • Heavy gaming can reduce battery faster at 144Hz mode
  • Keyboard accessories may require separate purchase

Verdict

The Lenovo Idea Tab Pro is one of the best Android tablets in India for buyers wanting premium multimedia, productivity, and gaming performance without entering ultra-premium flagship pricing.

Its biggest strengths are the large 3K 144Hz display, powerful Dimensity 8300 chipset, quad JBL speakers, and stylus support that make it ideal for students, professionals, gamers, and OTT streaming users. The massive battery and fast charging also improve long-term everyday usability.

While premium flagship tablets may still offer stronger ecosystem integration and app optimization, for overall value, entertainment, and productivity, the Idea Tab Pro delivers an excellent all-round tablet experience.

Best for:

  • Students and professionals
  • Note-taking and study usage
  • OTT streaming and entertainment
  • Android gaming
  • Creative and productivity tasks

Skip if:

  • You prefer compact lightweight tablets
  • You need professional iPad-level creative app ecosystem
  • You mainly use tablets for basic browsing only
