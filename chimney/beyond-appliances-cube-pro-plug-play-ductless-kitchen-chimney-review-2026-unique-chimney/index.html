Beyond Appliances Cube Pro Plug & Play Ductless Kitchen Chimney Review (2026) – A Unique Chimney for Homes Without Ducting

The Beyond Appliances Cube Pro takes a different approach from traditional kitchen chimneys. With its ductless design, activated charcoal filtration, low-noise operation, and plug-and-play installation, it aims to solve a common problem faced by apartment owners and renters who cannot install external ducting.

Verdict

The Beyond Appliances Cube Pro is not trying to compete directly with premium ducted chimneys from brands like Faber, Elica, or KAFF. Instead, it targets a specific audience—people who need a practical chimney solution without the hassle of duct installation.

If your kitchen doesn't allow ducting or you're living in a rented apartment, the Cube Pro is one of the more interesting alternatives currently available. The combination of ductless operation, low noise levels, auto-clean technology, and decent suction makes it a compelling option for modern urban homes.
