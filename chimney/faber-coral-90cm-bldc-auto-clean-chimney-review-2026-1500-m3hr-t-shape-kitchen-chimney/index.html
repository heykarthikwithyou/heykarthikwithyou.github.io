Verdict

The Faber Coral 90cm BLDC Auto-Clean Chimney is a premium solution for homeowners who need powerful smoke extraction, low maintenance, and advanced control features.

Its biggest strengths are the 1500 m³/hr suction capacity, BLDC motor efficiency, filterless auto-clean technology, and gesture-based controls. These features make it especially suitable for large kitchens and households that frequently prepare fried, grilled, or heavily seasoned Indian dishes.

Compared to many standard chimneys, the Coral BLDC offers stronger suction, better energy efficiency, and enhanced long-term reliability, making it an excellent investment for modern kitchens.

Best for:

  • Large Indian kitchens
  • 3-burner to 5-burner cooktops
  • Heavy daily cooking
  • Buyers seeking premium chimney features
  • Homeowners prioritizing low maintenance and long warranty coverage

Skip if:

  • You have a compact kitchen with limited installation space
  • You need a budget-friendly chimney solution
  • Your cooking generates minimal smoke and grease
