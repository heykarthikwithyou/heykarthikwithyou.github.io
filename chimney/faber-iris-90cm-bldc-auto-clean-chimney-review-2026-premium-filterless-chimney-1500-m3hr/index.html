Faber Iris 90cm BLDC Auto-Clean Chimney Review (2026) – Premium Filterless Chimney with 1500 m³/hr Suction

Verdict

The Faber Iris 90cm is a well-equipped premium chimney that combines strong suction, smart controls, and efficient operation. Its 1500 m³/hr airflow is more than capable of handling everyday Indian cooking, including deep frying, grilling, and frequent tadka preparation.

The BLDC motor is one of its biggest advantages, offering lower power consumption and potentially longer life compared to standard motors. Combined with the filterless design, auto-clean system, and 9-speed control, it delivers a user-friendly experience with less maintenance.

If you're looking for a premium 90cm chimney that balances performance, energy efficiency, and modern features, the Faber Iris is a strong choice for medium to large kitchens.
