Features

  • Ultra Soft Suede Fabric – Feels premium, smooth on skin, and suitable for long sitting hours (especially useful in Indian weather with fans/AC).
  • Manual Reclining Mechanism – Easy push-back reclining, no electricity needed.
  • Oversized Leg Rest – Proper leg support for taller users (a big plus compared to compact recliners).
  • Cushioned Lumbar Support – Helps reduce back strain during TV time or work-from-home breaks.
  • Single Seater Compact Design – Fits easily in 1BHK/2BHK homes without taking too much space.
  • Sturdy Frame Build – Designed for daily usage with decent durability.
  • Neutral Grey Color – Matches most Indian living room interiors.

Pros

  • Extremely comfortable for long sitting (TV, Netflix, mobile scrolling 😄)
  • Soft suede fabric feels premium at this price
  • Reclining is smooth and simple (no complex setup)
  • Good lumbar support – better than many budget recliners
  • Compact enough for Indian apartments
  • Value for money compared to branded showroom recliners

Cons

  • Manual recline may feel basic for some users
  • Suede fabric can attract dust (needs occasional cleaning)
  • Not ideal for very heavy users (check weight capacity before buying)
  • Assembly may take a bit of effort if you’re not used to DIY

Verdict

If you’re looking for a comfortable, affordable recliner for everyday use, the Green Soul Cloud is a solid choice in the Indian market.

It’s not a luxury recliner, but it nails the essentials—comfort, support, and usability. For people setting up a cozy living room, gaming corner, or even a relaxation spot after work, this offers great value without burning your budget.

👉 Best for:

  • Small to medium homes
  • Budget buyers
  • Daily relaxation / TV watching
  • Work-from-home comfort seating

👉 Skip if:

  • You want electric recline or premium luxury build
  • You prefer low-maintenance leather-type material
