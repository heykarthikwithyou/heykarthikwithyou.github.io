Verdict

The Prestige PIC 20 Induction Cooktop is a practical and reliable option for Indian households looking for fast, portable, and energy-efficient cooking.

Its biggest strengths are the preset Indian cooking menus, compact portability, and electrical safety features that make it highly suitable for students, bachelors, small families, and backup cooking setups. The induction heating also reduces kitchen heat and offers quicker cooking compared to traditional gas stoves.

While it requires compatible cookware and may not fully replace large multi-burner gas setups for bigger families, for daily convenience cooking and portable usage, it offers excellent overall value.

Best for:

  • Small families and bachelors
  • Hostel and PG users
  • Backup kitchen cooking
  • Fast everyday cooking
  • Portable cooking setups

Skip if:

  • You frequently cook using oversized utensils
  • You need multi-burner cooking flexibility
  • You do not have induction-compatible cookware
