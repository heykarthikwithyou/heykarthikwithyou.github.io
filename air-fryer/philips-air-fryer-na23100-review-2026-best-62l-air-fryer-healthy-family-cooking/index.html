Verdict

The Philips Air Fryer NA231/00 is an excellent air fryer for Indian households looking for healthier cooking, large family capacity, and premium usability features.

Its biggest strengths are the 6.2L capacity, Rapid Air Technology, and convenient cooking window that make it ideal for preparing snacks, fries, chicken, paneer, baked foods, and everyday meals with significantly less oil. The touch panel interface also improves overall user experience for modern kitchens.

While compact users may prefer smaller air fryers, for families wanting healthier cooking with strong performance and reliable brand quality, the Philips NA231/00 delivers excellent overall value.
