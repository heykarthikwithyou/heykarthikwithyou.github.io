POP X Review (2026) – Budget Smartphone with 120Hz Display and Rugged Protection?

A stylish entry-level smartphone featuring a smooth 120Hz display, durable design, AI-powered features, and long battery life for everyday Indian users.

Features

  • 6.74-inch 120Hz Smooth Display – Delivers smoother scrolling, gaming, and video experience compared to standard 60Hz phones.
  • 5000mAh Battery – Reliable all-day battery backup for calls, streaming, and social media usage.
  • IP64 Protection – Better resistance against dust and water splashes for daily durability.
  • Drop Resistant Design – Built to handle minor accidental drops and rough usage.
  • AI Active Noise Cancellation – Helps improve voice clarity during phone calls.
  • AI Song Identification – Quickly identifies songs playing nearby without needing separate apps.
  • AI Flash Snap Feature – Helps capture fast-moving moments more efficiently.
  • Chilly Green Design – Modern youthful color finish with attractive styling.
  • 4GB RAM + 64GB Storage – Suitable for basic multitasking and daily smartphone usage.

Pros

  • Smooth 120Hz display feels impressive in budget segment
  • Good battery backup for regular users
  • Durable design with IP64 protection adds reliability
  • AI features improve everyday convenience
  • Large display is good for entertainment and browsing
  • Attractive design and color finish
  • Value-for-money option for casual users

Cons

  • Camera performance may struggle in low light
  • Not designed for heavy gaming or power users
  • HD+ display resolution may not feel very sharp
  • 4GB RAM can feel limited for intensive multitasking
  • Charging speed may be average compared to rivals

Verdict

The POP X focuses on delivering a balanced experience for budget-conscious Indian buyers who want style, durability, and smooth everyday performance.

The 120Hz display and rugged protection features stand out in this segment, making it appealing for students, first-time smartphone users, and users upgrading from older entry-level devices. The AI-powered features also add some useful convenience rarely seen in budget phones.

It may not compete with premium devices in gaming or photography, but for daily calling, entertainment, browsing, and social media, the POP X offers solid value.

Best for:

  • Students and casual users
  • First-time smartphone buyers
  • Users wanting durable budget phones
  • Daily entertainment and social media usage

Skip if:

  • You want flagship-level camera quality
  • You do heavy gaming regularly
  • You need very high multitasking performance
