iQOO 15R Review (2026) – Ultimate Gaming Smartphone with Massive 7600mAh Battery?

Features

  • Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 Processor – Delivers flagship-level gaming and multitasking performance. 
  • 7600mAh Massive Battery – One of the biggest batteries in a slim premium smartphone. 
  • 144Hz AMOLED Display – Smooth scrolling and high-FPS gaming experience with vibrant visuals. 
  • 100W Fast Charging Support – Quickly charges despite the large battery size. 
  • 6.5K IceCore VC Cooling System – Helps maintain stable gaming performance during long sessions. 
  • LPDDR5X RAM & UFS 4.1 Storage – Faster app loading, gaming performance, and multitasking. 
  • 50MP Sony OIS Camera – Reliable primary camera with optical image stabilization. 
  • Premium Slim Design – Surprisingly slim body despite the huge battery capacity.

Pros

  • Exceptional gaming performance with stable FPS
  • Huge battery backup easily lasts more than a day
  • AMOLED display looks premium and ultra-smooth
  • Fast charging is genuinely impressive
  • Thermal management works well during gaming
  • Premium in-hand feel despite large battery
  • Excellent value compared to flagship gaming phones

Cons

  • Camera system focuses more on performance than photography
  • Slightly heavy for one-handed long-term usage
  • UI may include some pre-installed apps
  • Premium pricing compared to mid-range phones
  • Not ideal for users wanting compact smartphones

Verdict

The iQOO 15R is easily one of the strongest performance-focused smartphones available in India right now.

It combines flagship Snapdragon power, excellent thermal management, ultra-smooth gaming performance, and a massive 7600mAh battery in a surprisingly slim design. For gamers, heavy users, and power users, this phone delivers a near-flagship experience without crossing ultra-premium pricing territory.

The cameras are decent, but the real focus here is raw speed, battery life, and gaming stability—and in those areas, the iQOO 15R absolutely shines.

👉 Best for:

  • Mobile gamers
  • Heavy smartphone users
  • Content consumption and streaming
  • Users wanting long battery life
  • Performance-focused buyers

👉 Skip if:

  • Camera quality is your top priority
  • You prefer compact lightweight phones
  • You want a clean stock Android experience
