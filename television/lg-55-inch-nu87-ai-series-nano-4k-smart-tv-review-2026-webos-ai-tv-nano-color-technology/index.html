Features

  • 55-Inch (139 cm) 4K Ultra HD Display – Large-screen viewing experience with exceptional clarity and detail.
  • Nano Color Technology – Enhances color purity and accuracy for more realistic and vibrant visuals.
  • 4K Ultra HD Resolution – Delivers four times the detail of Full HD content for sharper picture quality.
  • AI-Powered Picture Optimization – Automatically adjusts picture settings based on content and viewing conditions.
  • webOS Smart TV Platform – Provides access to popular OTT apps, streaming services, and smart entertainment features.
  • AI Processor Technology – Improves image processing, upscaling, and overall viewing quality.
  • HDR Support – Enhances contrast, brightness, and color depth in supported content.
  • Voice Control Integration – Supports convenient voice commands for content search and TV control.
  • Multiple Connectivity Options – Includes HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and screen sharing capabilities.
  • Gaming-Friendly Features – Optimized settings for casual gaming and responsive gameplay.
  • Premium Slim Design – Modern aesthetics with minimal bezels for an immersive viewing experience.
  • Smart Home Compatibility – Integrates with connected smart devices and home ecosystems.

Pros

  • Large 55-inch screen ideal for immersive viewing
  • Nano Color technology enhances color reproduction
  • Reliable and user-friendly webOS platform
  • AI-powered picture optimization improves viewing quality
  • Excellent 4K content experience
  • Wide app and streaming service support
  • Premium design and build quality
  • Suitable for movies, sports, and family entertainment

Cons

  • Audio quality may benefit from a dedicated soundbar
  • Premium pricing compared to entry-level 4K TVs
  • VA panel viewing angles may be narrower than IPS alternatives
  • High-end OLED TVs still offer superior contrast performance
  • Advanced gaming features may vary compared to flagship gaming TVs

Verdict

The LG 55-Inch NU87 AI Series Nano 4K Smart TV is a strong premium choice for users seeking a large-screen entertainment experience with enhanced color accuracy and smart AI features.

Its biggest strengths are the Nano Color technology, AI-powered picture processing, webOS smart platform, and expansive 55-inch display. These features make it particularly well-suited for movie enthusiasts, sports fans, OTT streaming, and family entertainment.

Compared to conventional 4K LED TVs, the NU87 AI Series offers improved color performance and smarter image processing, making it an attractive option for buyers who want a more premium viewing experience without stepping into OLED pricing.

Best for:

  • Home theater enthusiasts
  • OTT streaming and binge watching
  • Sports viewing
  • Family entertainment
  • Buyers seeking a premium 55-inch 4K Smart TV

Skip if:

  • You want OLED-level contrast and black levels
  • You need professional-grade gaming features
  • You are shopping within a tight budget
