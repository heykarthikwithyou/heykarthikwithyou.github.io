TCL 65T8C 65-Inch 4K QLED Google TV Review (2026) – Big-Screen Gaming & OTT Entertainment on a Budget

Features

  • 65-Inch 4K Ultra HD QLED Panel – Large-screen immersive viewing experience with vibrant Quantum Dot colors.
  • 120Hz Native Refresh Rate with VRR 144Hz – Smooth motion handling for gaming and sports content. 
  • DLG 288Hz Game Accelerator – Improved gaming responsiveness for competitive console gaming. 
  • Dolby Vision & HDR10 Support – Enhanced HDR performance with better contrast and brighter highlights. 
  • AiPQ Processor – AI-powered picture optimization for sharper visuals and color enhancement. 
  • Google TV Platform – Access to Netflix, Prime Video, Hotstar, YouTube, Zee5, and thousands of apps.
  • Dolby Atmos Audio – Immersive surround sound support for movies and OTT streaming.
  • MEMC 120Hz Motion Clarity – Smoother playback during sports and fast-moving scenes. 
  • 3GB RAM + 32GB Storage – Faster app performance and smoother multitasking compared to many budget TVs. 
  • Game Master Features – Includes ALLM, VRR, and gaming-focused optimizations.
  • Metallic Bezel-Less Design – Premium modern appearance suitable for living room setups.
  • Voice Assistant & Smart Home Support – Works with Google Assistant and Alexa-enabled devices.

Pros

  • Excellent value-for-money large-screen QLED TV
  • Strong gaming features with 144Hz support
  • Bright and vibrant QLED picture quality
  • Google TV interface feels modern and smooth
  • Good HDR performance for movies and OTT apps
  • 3GB RAM improves responsiveness noticeably
  • Dolby Atmos support enhances entertainment experience
  • Large 65-inch screen feels immersive for Indian living rooms

Cons

  • Black levels are not as deep as OLED or Mini LED TVs
  • Built-in speakers are decent but external soundbar improves experience
  • Some blooming and panel uniformity issues can appear in darker scenes 
  • TCL after-sales experience can vary by location
  • Viewing angles are decent but not flagship-level premium

Verdict

The TCL 65T8C is one of the strongest value-for-money 65-inch QLED TVs currently available in India for buyers who want premium gaming and OTT features without paying flagship pricing.

Its biggest strengths are the 144Hz gaming support, QLED color performance, Google TV platform, and smooth overall performance that make it ideal for PS5 gaming, IPL streaming, movies, and family entertainment. The 65-inch display size also creates a much more cinematic experience for Indian living rooms compared to smaller TVs.

Community discussions around TCL TVs also highlight that TCL offers aggressive features and panel technology at prices where Samsung, Sony, and LG usually offer more basic LED TVs.

While Mini LED and OLED TVs still provide better black levels and premium HDR performance, for buyers wanting a balanced large-screen entertainment TV under a reasonable budget, the TCL 65T8C delivers excellent overall value.

Best for:

  • PS5 and Xbox gamers
  • OTT binge watching
  • Sports and IPL streaming
  • Large living room entertainment
  • Buyers wanting premium features at lower pricing

Skip if:

  • You want OLED-level blacks and contrast
  • You prioritize premium brand after-sales service
  • You need flagship-level audio performance without soundbars
