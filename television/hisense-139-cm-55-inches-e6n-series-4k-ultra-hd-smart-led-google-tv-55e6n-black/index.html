Hisense 139 cm (55 inches) E6N Series 4K Ultra HD Smart LED Google TV 55E6N (Black)

A value-for-money 4K Google TV with Dolby Vision, smooth smart features, and immersive entertainment experience designed for Indian families.

Features

  • 55-inch 4K Ultra HD Display – Delivers sharp visuals, vibrant colors, and better clarity for movies and OTT content.
  • Google TV Platform – Access Netflix, Prime Video, YouTube, JioHotstar, SonyLIV, Zee5, and thousands of apps.
  • Dolby Vision & HDR10 Support – Enhances contrast and color quality for more immersive viewing.
  • Dolby Audio & DTS Virtual:X – Improves overall sound experience for movies, sports, and music.
  • MEMC Technology – Reduces motion blur during sports and fast-action scenes.
  • Game Mode Plus with ALLM & VRR – Better responsiveness for casual console gaming.
  • Chromecast & AirPlay Support – Easily cast content from Android, iPhone, and laptops.
  • 24W Speaker Output – Loud and clear audio suitable for regular room usage.
  • Wide Viewing Angles – Good picture visibility from different seating positions.
  • Multiple Connectivity Options – Includes HDMI, USB, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and Ethernet support. 

Pros

  • Excellent value for a 55-inch 4K Google TV
  • Dolby Vision improves OTT viewing experience
  • Google TV interface feels smooth and user-friendly
  • Good color performance for movies and YouTube content
  • Useful gaming features for casual gamers
  • Supports almost all major OTT apps in India
  • Modern slim-bezel design looks premium

Cons

  • 60Hz panel is not ideal for competitive gaming
  • Sound quality is decent but improves significantly with a soundbar
  • Black levels are average compared to premium QLED or OLED TVs
  • Upscaling of low-quality cable channels could be better
  • UI performance may slow slightly over long-term heavy usage

Verdict

The Hisense 55E6N is one of the better budget-friendly 55-inch Google TVs available for Indian buyers who want a large-screen 4K entertainment experience without spending premium flagship TV money.

Its strengths are clearly in content consumption — OTT streaming, YouTube, cricket, movies, and casual gaming all look enjoyable thanks to Dolby Vision support and the Google TV interface. The addition of MEMC, VRR, and ALLM also makes it more feature-rich than many similarly priced competitors.

While it cannot match premium Sony or Samsung TVs in processing and deep contrast performance, for its price segment, the Hisense E6N offers excellent overall value and a strong feature set.

Best for:

  • OTT streaming and movies
  • Family living rooms
  • Budget-conscious 55-inch TV buyers
  • Casual gaming and sports viewing

Skip if:

  • You want flagship-level picture processing
  • You need 120Hz gaming support
  • You prefer premium QLED or OLED picture quality
