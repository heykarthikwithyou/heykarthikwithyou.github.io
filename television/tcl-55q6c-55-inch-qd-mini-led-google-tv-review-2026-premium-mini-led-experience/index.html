TCL 55Q6C 55-Inch QD-Mini LED Google TV Review (2026) – Premium Mini LED Experience at a Competitive Price

Features

  • 55-Inch 4K UHD QD-Mini LED Display – Delivers deeper blacks, brighter highlights, and better contrast compared to standard LED TVs.
  • QD-Mini LED Technology – Combines Quantum Dot colors with Mini LED backlighting for improved picture quality.
  • Up to 512 Dimming Zones – Better local dimming helps improve HDR performance and black levels. 
  • 144Hz Native Refresh Rate – Smooth gaming and motion clarity for PS5, Xbox, and fast-action sports content. 
  • Dolby Vision IQ & HDR10 Support – Enhanced HDR viewing with improved brightness and cinematic visuals.
  • AiPQ Pro Processor – AI-based picture optimization for better colors, sharpness, and motion handling. 
  • Google TV OS – Access to Netflix, Prime Video, YouTube, Hotstar, Zee5, and thousands of apps.
  • Dolby Atmos Audio – Immersive sound experience with cinematic audio support.
  • Game Master Features – Includes ALLM, AMD FreeSync Premium Pro, and low-latency gaming optimizations. 
  • Hands-Free Voice Control – Google Assistant support for voice commands and smart home integration.
  • AirPlay 2 & Alexa Compatibility – Flexible ecosystem support for Apple and smart home users.
  • Slim Premium Design – Modern near-borderless look suitable for living rooms and entertainment setups.

Pros

  • Excellent picture quality for the price segment
  • Mini LED technology improves contrast significantly
  • 144Hz refresh rate is great for gaming
  • Strong HDR performance with Dolby Vision IQ
  • Google TV interface feels smooth and modern
  • Good value compared to premium Samsung and Sony Mini LED TVs
  • Gaming performance works well for PS5 and Xbox users
  • Bright display performs well in Indian living rooms

Cons

  • Built-in speakers are decent but external soundbars improve experience
  • TCL after-sales service can vary depending on city location
  • Black uniformity may not match premium OLED TVs
  • Some users may notice blooming in extreme HDR scenes
  • 2-star energy rating may concern power-conscious buyers

Verdict

The TCL 55Q6C is one of the best value-for-money Mini LED TVs available in India for buyers who want premium picture quality without paying flagship Samsung or Sony pricing.

Its biggest strengths are the QD-Mini LED panel, 144Hz gaming support, Dolby Vision IQ, and strong HDR brightness that make it excellent for movies, OTT streaming, sports, and console gaming. For PS5 and Xbox users especially, the gaming performance and low input lag make it a very attractive option in the under-₹50K to ₹60K segment.

Community feedback from Indian users has also been largely positive, especially regarding gaming and movie performance for the price.

While OLED TVs still deliver better absolute black levels, the TCL Q6C offers a fantastic balance of brightness, gaming performance, smart features, and pricing that is hard to beat in this segment.

Best for:

  • PS5 and Xbox gaming
  • OTT streaming and movies
  • Bright living rooms
  • Buyers wanting Mini LED quality on a budget
  • Sports and fast-motion content

Skip if:

  • You want flagship OLED-level blacks
  • You prioritize premium brand after-sales support
  • You need ultra-premium built-in audio quality
