Features

  • 46dB Hybrid Active Noise Cancellation – Helps reduce surrounding noise for a more immersive listening experience. 
  • 12.4mm Dynamic Bass Driver – Large drivers designed to deliver deeper bass and energetic sound output. 
  • 360° Spatial Audio Support – Creates a wider and more immersive surround-style audio experience. 
  • Up to 40 Hours Total Battery Life – Long playback support with charging case included. 
  • Fast Charging Support – 10 minutes of charging provides up to 5 hours of playback. 
  • Ultra Low Latency Gaming Mode – Around 45ms latency improves gaming audio synchronization. 
  • Bluetooth 5.4 Connectivity – Stable wireless connection with improved efficiency and compatibility. 
  • Dual Device Connection – Easily switch between smartphone, tablet, or laptop usage. 
  • IP55 Water & Dust Resistance – Better durability against sweat and light splashes. 
  • Touch Control Support – Quick access to music, calls, and gaming controls.
  • Comfortable Lightweight Fit – Suitable for long listening and gaming sessions.
  • Stylish Vibrant Black Finish – Modern premium-looking compact design.

Pros

  • Strong ANC performance for the price segment
  • Bass-heavy sound feels energetic and immersive
  • Excellent battery backup for daily usage
  • Fast charging feature is highly convenient
  • Low latency mode benefits mobile gaming
  • Bluetooth 5.4 connectivity feels stable
  • Dual-device support improves productivity usage
  • Good value-for-money TWS earbuds option

Cons

  • Sound tuning may feel bass-heavy for audiophiles
  • Spatial audio feature may not appeal to all listeners
  • No advanced LDAC or Hi-Res codec support mentioned by users 
  • Some users reported occasional connectivity or sync issues over long-term usage 
  • Glossy finishes may attract scratches more easily on lighter variants 

Verdict

The realme Buds T310 are among the strongest budget ANC earbuds for users looking for powerful bass, long battery life, gaming-friendly performance, and modern wireless features at an affordable price.

Their biggest strengths are the 46dB Hybrid ANC, immersive bass-focused audio, low latency gaming mode, and excellent battery backup that make them ideal for students, commuters, casual gamers, and entertainment-focused users. Community feedback especially praises the battery life, comfort, and feature-to-price ratio.

While audiophiles seeking neutral sound tuning and premium codec support may prefer higher-end alternatives, for everyday music, gaming, calls, and multimedia usage, the realme Buds T310 deliver excellent overall value in the budget TWS category.

Best for:

  • Budget ANC earbud buyers
  • Mobile gaming
  • Bass-heavy music lovers
  • Students and commuters
  • Everyday entertainment usage

Skip if:

  • You prefer neutral audiophile-grade sound
  • You need premium lossless codec support
  • You want flagship-level ANC refinement
