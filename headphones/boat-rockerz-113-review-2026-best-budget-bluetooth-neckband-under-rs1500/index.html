Features

  • Up to 40 Hours Battery Life – Long-lasting playback for travel, office, and daily usage.
  • Fast Charging Support – Quick charge feature gives hours of playback in just a few minutes.
  • ENx Technology for Calls – Reduces background noise during voice calls for clearer conversations.
  • Dual Pairing Feature – Connect two devices simultaneously, useful for laptop + phone usage.
  • Magnetic Earbuds – Prevents tangling and makes carrying easier.
  • Bluetooth Wireless Connectivity – Stable wireless performance with smartphones, tablets, and laptops.
  • App Support for Music Streaming Features – Added controls and customization support through companion app.
  • Lightweight Neckband Design – Comfortable for long listening sessions.

Pros

  • Excellent battery backup for the price
  • Comfortable fit for daily use
  • Fast charging is genuinely useful
  • Good call quality for work and online meetings
  • Dual pairing works smoothly
  • Lightweight and travel-friendly

Cons

  • Bass-heavy tuning may not suit everyone
  • Sound clarity is decent but not audiophile-level
  • Plastic build feels average
  • No active noise cancellation (ANC)
  • Magnetic lock could be stronger

Verdict

The boAt Rockerz 113 is a strong choice for users looking for a budget-friendly Bluetooth neckband with reliable battery life and calling performance.

For everyday usage like music, YouTube, online classes, work calls, and commuting, it delivers more than expected at this price range. The 40-hour battery backup is one of its biggest highlights, especially for people who hate charging devices frequently.

If your priority is practicality, comfort, and battery life over premium sound quality, this neckband is worth considering.
