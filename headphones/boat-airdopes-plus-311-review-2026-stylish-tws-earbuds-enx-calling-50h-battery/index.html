Verdict

The boAt Airdopes Plus 311 are stylish and practical budget wireless earbuds designed for users looking for long battery life, modern design, and reliable everyday audio performance.

Their biggest strengths are the premium-looking glass-inspired styling, 50-hour battery backup, ENx calling support, and lightweight comfort that make them suitable for students, commuters, casual music listeners, and work-from-home users. The fast charging support also improves convenience for regular daily usage.

While users wanting premium ANC and studio-grade audio may prefer higher-end earbuds, for affordable everyday entertainment and calling performance, the Airdopes Plus 311 deliver strong overall value.

Best for:

  • Students and commuters
  • Budget wireless earbud buyers
  • Users wanting long battery backup

Skip if:
