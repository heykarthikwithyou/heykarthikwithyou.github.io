Lenovo ThinkBook 16 Ryzen 5 Laptop Review (2026) – Powerful Thin & Light Laptop for Work, Coding & Multitasking

A premium 16-inch business-style laptop with Ryzen 5 HS-series performance, 16GB RAM, aluminium build, and productivity-focused features designed for professionals, students, and creators.

Features

  • AMD Ryzen 5 7535HS Processor – Powerful HS-series CPU suitable for multitasking, coding, office work, and light creative workloads.
  • 16GB DDR5 RAM – Smooth performance during heavy browser usage, productivity tasks, and multitasking.
  • 512GB SSD Storage – Fast boot times and quick application loading performance.
  • 16-inch WUXGA IPS Display – Large 16:10 screen provides more workspace for productivity and entertainment.
  • 300 Nits Brightness – Comfortable indoor visibility for work and media consumption.
  • Premium Aluminium Top Design – Gives the laptop a more professional and durable appearance.
  • Backlit Keyboard – Useful for night-time work and low-light environments.
  • Fingerprint Scanner – Faster and more secure login experience.
  • Windows 11 Home + Office 2024 – Ready-to-use productivity setup out of the box.
  • Thin & Lightweight Build – Around 1.7kg weight makes it portable for office and college use.
  • Multiple Connectivity Options – Includes USB-C, HDMI, USB-A, Wi-Fi 6E, and Ethernet support.

Pros

  • Strong Ryzen HS-series performance for productivity
  • Large 16-inch display improves multitasking comfort
  • Premium aluminium design feels professional
  • 16GB RAM is ideal for modern workloads
  • Comfortable keyboard for typing and coding
  • Good port selection including USB-C and Ethernet
  • Fingerprint scanner adds convenience and security
  • Suitable for coding, office work, studies, and light editing

Cons

  • Integrated graphics are not ideal for heavy gaming
  • 45% NTSC panel is average for professional color work
  • Battery life is decent but not exceptional for heavy users
  • Speakers are adequate but not premium-level
  • Display resolution is good, but QHD would feel sharper on 16 inches

Verdict

The Lenovo ThinkBook 16 Ryzen 5 7535HS is an excellent productivity-focused laptop for Indian buyers who want a balance of performance, portability, and professional design without entering premium ultrabook pricing.

Its biggest strengths are the powerful HS-series Ryzen processor, 16GB RAM, large 16-inch display, and business-friendly features like the fingerprint scanner and aluminium build. It performs especially well for coding, office work, business usage, multitasking, online classes, web development, and light content creation.

While it is not built for high-end gaming or professional color-accurate creative workflows, for students, developers, office professionals, and multitaskers, it offers excellent real-world usability and long-term value.

Best for:

  • Office and work-from-home users
  • Programming and web development
  • Students and productivity users
  • Multitasking and business usage

Skip if:

  • You want dedicated GPU gaming performance
  • You need professional-grade color accuracy
  • You want ultra-premium OLED display quality
