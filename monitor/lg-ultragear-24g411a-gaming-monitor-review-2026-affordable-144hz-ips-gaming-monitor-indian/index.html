LG UltraGear 24G411A Gaming Monitor Review (2026) – Affordable 144Hz IPS Gaming Monitor for Indian Gamers

A fast and stylish 24-inch gaming monitor with IPS panel, 144Hz refresh rate, HDR10 support, and smooth gameplay features designed for esports, casual gaming, and everyday use.

Features

  • 24-inch Full HD IPS Display – Delivers sharp visuals, vibrant colors, and wide viewing angles.
  • 1920 × 1080 Resolution – Suitable for gaming, streaming, office work, and daily usage.
  • 144Hz Refresh Rate (Overclocked) – Provides smoother gameplay experience during fast-action gaming.
  • 1ms Motion Blur Reduction – Reduces ghosting and motion blur in competitive games.
  • NVIDIA G-Sync Compatible – Helps minimize screen tearing and stuttering.
  • AMD FreeSync Support – Improves smoothness during gaming sessions.
  • HDR10 Support – Enhances contrast and color depth for supported games and content.
  • 99% sRGB Color Coverage – Better color reproduction for gaming and multimedia usage.
  • Anti-Glare Screen – Reduces reflections and improves visibility in bright rooms.
  • HDMI, DisplayPort & Headphone Out – Useful connectivity options for gaming setups.
  • Slim UltraGear Design – Modern gaming aesthetics with minimal bezels.
  • Tilt Adjustable Stand – Basic ergonomic adjustment for comfortable usage.

Pros

  • Smooth 144Hz gameplay experience
  • IPS panel offers good colors and viewing angles
  • G-Sync and FreeSync support improve gaming smoothness
  • HDR10 support enhances media experience
  • Anti-glare coating helps during long sessions
  • Good value-for-money gaming monitor
  • Suitable for both gaming and daily productivity

Cons

  • Full HD resolution may feel less sharp for some users on 24-inch screens
  • Stand adjustment is limited to tilt only
  • HDR performance is basic compared to premium HDR monitors
  • Built-in speakers are not included
  • Peak brightness is average for brightly lit rooms 

Verdict

The LG UltraGear 24G411A is a strong budget-friendly gaming monitor for Indian buyers looking for smooth gameplay, reliable IPS panel quality, and essential gaming features without spending heavily.

Its biggest strengths are the 144Hz refresh rate, responsive gaming performance, and balanced color quality that make it suitable for competitive gaming, casual AAA gaming, streaming, and even daily work usage. The addition of G-Sync compatibility and FreeSync also improves the overall gaming experience significantly compared to standard 60Hz monitors.

While it does not offer premium HDR performance or advanced ergonomic adjustments, for its price segment, it delivers excellent overall value and is a great upgrade from basic office monitors.

Best for:

  • Esports and competitive gaming
  • Budget gaming setups
  • Students and work-from-home users
  • Buyers upgrading from 60Hz monitors

Skip if:

  • You want QHD or 4K resolution clarity
  • You need advanced height-adjustable stands
  • You prefer premium HDR gaming monitors
