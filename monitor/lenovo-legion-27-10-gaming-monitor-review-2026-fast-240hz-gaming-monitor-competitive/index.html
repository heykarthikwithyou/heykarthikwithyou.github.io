Lenovo Legion 27-10 Gaming Monitor Review (2026) – Fast 240Hz Gaming Monitor for Competitive Players

A high-refresh-rate 27-inch IPS gaming monitor with 240Hz smoothness, ultra-fast response time, and ergonomic adjustments built for competitive gaming and esports performance.

Features

  • 27-inch Full HD IPS Display – Offers wide viewing angles and vibrant color reproduction.
  • 240Hz Refresh Rate – Extremely smooth gameplay experience for competitive gaming.
  • 0.5ms Response Time – Reduces motion blur and ghosting during fast-action gameplay.
  • AMD FreeSync & NVIDIA G-Sync Support – Minimizes screen tearing and stuttering.
  • 99% sRGB Color Coverage – Delivers accurate and rich colors for gaming and media consumption.
  • HDMI 2.1 & DisplayPort 1.4 Connectivity – Modern ports for gaming PCs and consoles.
  • Ergonomic Stand Design – Supports tilt, swivel, pivot, and height adjustments for better comfort.
  • Ultra-Slim Gaming Design – Premium Legion styling suitable for modern gaming setups.
  • IPS Panel Technology – Better color consistency compared to many TN esports monitors.
  • Smooth Motion Handling – Excellent for FPS, racing, and fast-paced competitive games.

Pros

  • Extremely smooth 240Hz gaming experience
  • Fast response time improves competitive gameplay
  • IPS panel offers better visuals than TN alternatives
  • Ergonomic stand improves long-hour comfort
  • G-Sync and FreeSync support work well
  • Good color reproduction for gaming and content viewing
  • Premium gaming design and build quality

Cons

  • Full HD resolution may feel less sharp at 27 inches for some users
  • Requires powerful GPU to fully utilize 240Hz gaming
  • Built-in speakers may not be included in some variants
  • Premium pricing compared to standard 144Hz monitors
  • Not ideal for professional 4K content creation workflows

Verdict

The Lenovo Legion 27-10 is an excellent gaming monitor for Indian buyers focused on competitive gaming and ultra-smooth performance.

Its biggest strengths are the 240Hz refresh rate, ultra-fast response time, and IPS panel quality that together create a responsive and visually pleasing gaming experience. The ergonomic stand and modern connectivity options also make it practical for long gaming sessions and dual-purpose productivity setups.

While the Full HD resolution may not satisfy users seeking ultra-sharp visuals, for esports players and fast-paced gaming enthusiasts, the focus on speed and responsiveness makes this monitor a strong choice.

Best for:

  • Competitive FPS and esports gaming
  • High-refresh-rate gaming setups
  • Long gaming sessions
  • Users wanting smooth responsive gameplay

Skip if:

  • You prefer QHD or 4K resolution monitors
  • You mainly use monitors for professional editing work
  • You are on a limited gaming setup budget
