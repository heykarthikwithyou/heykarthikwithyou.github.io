Verdict

The Jam & Honey Kick Scooter is a practical and affordable option for parents looking to introduce children to outdoor riding activities.

Its biggest strengths are the foldable design, adjustable handlebar height, lightweight frame, and BIS-certified construction that make it suitable for everyday recreation, neighborhood rides, and skill development. The scooter also encourages physical activity while helping improve balance and coordination.

While older teenagers or advanced riders may eventually outgrow its 50kg capacity, for children between 6 and 13 years of age, this scooter offers excellent value and usability.

Best for:

  • Children aged 6–13 years
  • Beginner scooter riders
  • Outdoor recreation and play
  • Parents seeking a foldable scooter
  • Kids developing balance and coordination

Skip if:

  • The rider weighs more than 50kg
  • You need a stunt scooter for tricks
  • You want an electric scooter for commuting
