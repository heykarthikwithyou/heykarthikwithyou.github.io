Verdict

The L'Oréal Professionnel Xtenso Care Shampoo & Hair Masque Combo is one of the best maintenance routines for people with straightened, rebonded, smoothened, or chemically treated hair.

Its biggest strengths are the sulfate-free cleansing formula, deep-conditioning masque, and frizz-control benefits that help maintain smoothness, softness, and shine between salon visits. The combination is particularly effective for users looking to extend the lifespan of hair straightening treatments while minimizing dryness and damage.

Compared to standard shampoos, Xtenso Care offers a more specialized approach for treated hair, making it a worthwhile investment for maintaining salon-finished results at home.

Best for:

  • Users wanting smoother and shinier hair

Skip if:
