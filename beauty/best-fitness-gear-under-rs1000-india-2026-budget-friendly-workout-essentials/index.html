How to Choose the Right Fitness Gear

When selecting fitness gear under ₹1,000, focus on your primary fitness goal—whether it’s strength, cardio, flexibility, or general fitness. For strength training, dumbbells or resistance kits are ideal, while cardio enthusiasts should consider skipping ropes. Choosing the right category ensures better results and consistency.

Also, consider factors like durability, ease of use, and space requirements. Compact and multi-purpose equipment offers better value, especially for home workouts. Investing in versatile tools helps you build a balanced fitness routine without overspending.
