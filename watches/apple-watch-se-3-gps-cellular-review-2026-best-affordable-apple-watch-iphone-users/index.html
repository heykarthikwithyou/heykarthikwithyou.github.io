Apple Watch SE 3 GPS + Cellular Review (2026) – Best Affordable Apple Watch for iPhone Users?

A premium smartwatch with cellular connectivity, smooth performance, fitness tracking, and Apple ecosystem features—without the ultra-premium price tag.

Features

  • GPS + Cellular Connectivity – Make calls, receive messages, and stream music even without carrying your iPhone.
  • 44mm Retina Display – Bright, sharp, and easy-to-read screen for notifications, workouts, and apps.
  • Powerful Apple Silicon Performance – Smooth navigation, faster app loading, and responsive experience.
  • Advanced Fitness Tracking – Tracks steps, calories, heart rate, sleep, workouts, and daily activity.
  • Crash Detection & Emergency SOS – Useful safety features for emergencies and travel.
  • Swim & Water Resistant Design – Suitable for workouts, rain, and swimming sessions.
  • Midnight Aluminium Build – Lightweight yet premium-looking design for everyday wear.
  • Seamless Apple Ecosystem Integration – Works perfectly with iPhone, AirPods, Apple Music, and other Apple services.

Pros

  • Cellular feature adds real convenience
  • Smooth and reliable performance
  • Excellent fitness and health tracking
  • Premium design with comfortable fit
  • Strong app ecosystem compared to Android watches
  • Great for calls and notifications on the go
  • Easier on battery than flagship Apple Watch Ultra models

Cons

  • Works only with iPhones
  • Battery life still requires daily charging
  • No always-on display like premium Apple Watch models
  • Expensive compared to many Android smartwatches
  • Some advanced health features are missing

Verdict

The Apple Watch SE 3 GPS + Cellular is one of the best smartwatches for iPhone users who want the Apple experience without paying flagship prices.

It handles the essentials extremely well—fitness tracking, notifications, calling, safety features, and ecosystem integration. The cellular support is especially useful for workouts, bike rides, or quick outings where you don’t want to carry your phone.

While it misses a few premium features found in the higher-end Apple Watch models, for most Indian users, the SE 3 already delivers an excellent smartwatch experience.

👉 Best for:

  • iPhone users
  • Fitness and health tracking
  • Professionals and students
  • Users wanting cellular smartwatch convenience
  • First-time Apple Watch buyers

👉 Skip if:

  • You use an Android phone
  • You need multi-day battery life
  • You want advanced health sensors and premium display features
