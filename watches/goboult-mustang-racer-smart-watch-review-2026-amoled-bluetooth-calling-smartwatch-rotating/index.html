Features

  • 1.43-Inch AMOLED Display – Delivers vibrant colors, deep blacks, and sharp visuals for an enhanced viewing experience.
  • Rotating Bezel Design – Provides intuitive navigation and a premium watch-like feel.
  • Bluetooth Calling Support – Make and receive calls directly from the smartwatch when connected to a smartphone.
  • 500 Nits Brightness – Ensures good visibility indoors and outdoors under varying lighting conditions.
  • 150+ Watch Faces – Offers extensive customization options to match different styles and preferences.
  • AI Voice Assistant – Enables voice commands for quick access to functions and information.
  • SpO2 Monitoring – Tracks blood oxygen levels for wellness insights and daily health awareness.
  • Heart Rate Monitoring – Continuously monitors heart rate throughout the day.
  • 120+ Sports Modes – Supports tracking for a wide range of fitness activities and workouts.
  • Smart Notifications – Displays call alerts, messages, app notifications, and reminders.
  • Fitness Activity Tracking – Monitors steps, calories burned, distance, and activity goals.
  • Premium Apex Blue Finish – Sporty and modern design suitable for casual and professional use.

Pros

  • Bright and colorful AMOLED display
  • Rotating bezel improves navigation experience
  • Bluetooth calling adds everyday convenience
  • Supports a large number of sports modes
  • Attractive premium design
  • AI voice assistant enhances usability
  • Extensive watch face customization options
  • Good value-for-money smartwatch features

Cons

  • Battery life varies depending on calling and display usage
  • Health tracking features are not medical-grade tools
  • Third-party app support may be limited compared to premium smartwatches
  • AMOLED always-on features can reduce battery life
  • Advanced fitness metrics may be less detailed than dedicated fitness watches

Verdict

The GOBOULT Mustang Racer Smart Watch is an appealing option for users seeking a premium-looking smartwatch with modern features at an affordable price point.

Its biggest strengths are the AMOLED display, rotating bezel, Bluetooth calling functionality, and extensive fitness tracking options. These features make it suitable for students, professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and first-time smartwatch buyers looking for a balance of style and functionality.

For users who want a smartwatch that delivers a premium appearance, health monitoring, sports tracking, and smart connectivity without spending on flagship wearables, the Mustang Racer offers excellent overall value.

Best for:

  • Everyday smartwatch users
  • Fitness and activity tracking
  • Bluetooth calling convenience
  • Students and working professionals
  • Buyers seeking a premium-looking smartwatch on a budget

Skip if:

  • You need advanced medical-grade health features
  • You want a premium smartwatch ecosystem with extensive app support
  • You require multi-week battery life
