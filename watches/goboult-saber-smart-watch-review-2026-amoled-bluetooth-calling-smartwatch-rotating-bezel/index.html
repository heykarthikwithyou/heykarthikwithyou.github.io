Features

  • 1.43-Inch AMOLED Display – Delivers vibrant colors, deep blacks, and sharp visuals for an enhanced viewing experience.
  • Functional Rotating Bezel – Enables smooth navigation through menus, notifications, and smartwatch functions.
  • Bluetooth Calling Support – Make and receive calls directly from the watch when paired with a smartphone.
  • Always-On Display (AOD) – Allows convenient access to time and essential information without waking the screen.
  • 700 Nits Brightness – Provides clear visibility in both indoor and outdoor environments.
  • 200+ Watch Faces – Extensive customization options to match different styles and preferences.
  • AI Voice Assistant – Supports voice commands for improved convenience and hands-free operation.
  • SpO2 Monitoring – Tracks blood oxygen levels for daily wellness awareness.
  • Heart Rate Monitoring – Continuously monitors heart rate throughout the day.
  • 120+ Sports Modes – Supports a wide range of fitness activities and workout tracking.
  • Smart Notifications – Displays calls, messages, reminders, and app alerts directly on the wrist.
  • Premium Titanium-Inspired Design – Modern and sophisticated appearance suitable for both work and casual use.

Pros

  • Large AMOLED display with vibrant visuals
  • Functional rotating bezel improves navigation
  • Bluetooth calling enhances convenience
  • Always-On Display support included
  • 200+ watch face customization options
  • AI voice assistant adds smart functionality
  • Supports over 120 sports modes
  • Attractive premium-looking design

Cons

  • Battery life varies based on calling and AOD usage
  • Health tracking features are not medical-grade tools
  • Limited third-party app ecosystem compared to flagship smartwatches
  • AMOLED display features can increase power consumption
  • Advanced fitness analytics may be less detailed than dedicated fitness watches

Verdict

The GOBOULT Saber Smart Watch is a feature-rich wearable aimed at users who want a premium design and modern smartwatch functionality without paying flagship prices.

Its biggest strengths are the AMOLED display, functional rotating bezel, Bluetooth calling, Always-On Display, and extensive fitness tracking capabilities. These features make it suitable for professionals, students, fitness enthusiasts, and users seeking a stylish smartwatch for everyday use.

For buyers looking for a balance of design, smart features, and health tracking at an affordable price point, the GOBOULT Saber offers strong value and versatility.

Best for:

  • Everyday smartwatch users
  • Fitness and activity tracking
  • Bluetooth calling convenience
  • Professionals and students
  • Buyers seeking a premium-looking smartwatch on a budget

Skip if:

  • You need advanced ECG or medical-grade health features
  • You want a flagship smartwatch ecosystem with extensive apps
  • You require multi-week battery life on a single charge
