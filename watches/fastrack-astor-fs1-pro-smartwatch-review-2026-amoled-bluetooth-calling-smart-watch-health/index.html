Fastrack Astor FS1 Pro Smartwatch Review (2026) – AMOLED Bluetooth Calling Smart Watch with Health Tracking

Features

  • 1.97-Inch AMOLED Display – Large vibrant screen with sharp visuals, deep blacks, and excellent outdoor visibility.
  • Bluetooth Calling Support – Make and receive calls directly from the smartwatch when connected to a smartphone.
  • Functional Rotating Crown – Enhances navigation and provides a premium smartwatch experience.
  • 100+ Sports Modes – Tracks a wide variety of workouts, fitness activities, and sports sessions.
  • Heart Rate Monitoring – Helps users monitor heart rate throughout the day.
  • SpO2 Monitoring – Tracks blood oxygen saturation levels for wellness insights.
  • Women's Health Tracking – Includes features designed to support menstrual cycle and wellness tracking.
  • IP68 Water & Dust Resistance – Offers protection against sweat, splashes, dust, and everyday exposure.
  • Up to 5 Days Battery Life – Designed to provide multiple days of usage on a single charge.
  • Smart Notifications – Receive call alerts, messages, app notifications, and reminders on your wrist.
  • Fitness & Activity Tracking – Monitors daily steps, calories burned, and activity goals.
  • Stylish Premium Design – Modern aesthetic suitable for work, fitness, and casual wear.

Pros

  • Large and vibrant AMOLED display
  • Bluetooth calling works conveniently for daily use
  • Functional crown improves navigation experience
  • Comprehensive fitness and health tracking features
  • Supports over 100 sports modes
  • IP68 rating adds durability for everyday usage
  • Stylish design suitable for various occasions
  • Good value-for-money smartwatch in its segment

Cons

  • Battery life varies based on calling and display usage
  • Health metrics are useful for wellness tracking but not medical diagnosis
  • Limited third-party app ecosystem compared to premium smartwatches
  • GPS functionality may depend on connected smartphone in some activities
  • AMOLED display can consume more power with always-on features enabled

Verdict

The Fastrack Astor FS1 Pro is an excellent budget-to-midrange smartwatch for users looking for premium display quality, Bluetooth calling, and comprehensive fitness tracking without spending on flagship smartwatches.

Its biggest strengths are the large AMOLED display, Bluetooth calling support, extensive sports tracking, and modern design that make it suitable for students, professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and first-time smartwatch buyers. The addition of health monitoring features and IP68 protection further enhances everyday usability.

For users seeking a stylish smartwatch that balances smart features, fitness tracking, and affordability, the Astor FS1 Pro delivers strong overall value.

Best for:

  • Everyday smartwatch users
  • Fitness and activity tracking
  • Bluetooth calling convenience
  • Students and professionals
  • Buyers seeking an AMOLED smartwatch on a budget

Skip if:

  • You need advanced ECG or medical-grade health features
  • You want premium smartwatch app ecosystems
  • You require multi-week battery life
