  • Medium Size 68cm Check-in Luggage – Suitable for short vacations, business trips, and family travel.
  • Hard Case Polypropylene (PP) Shell – Durable outer body designed to handle rough travel conditions.
  • 360° 8-Wheel Spinner System – Smooth multi-directional movement for easier airport handling.
  • Lightweight Yet Strong Construction – Balances portability and durability effectively.
  • Integrated Combination Lock – Adds better luggage security during travel.
  • Spacious Interior Storage – Suitable for clothes, travel essentials, and accessories.
  • Organized Compartment Layout – Helps with easier packing and item separation.
  • Telescopic Trolley Handle – Adjustable handle for comfortable movement.
  • Scratch-Resistant Exterior Finish – Helps maintain cleaner appearance during regular travel.
  • Stylish Blue Frog Color Design – Modern and vibrant luggage appearance.

Pros

  • Durable polypropylene shell offers good protection
  • Smooth 8-wheel movement improves travel comfort
  • Medium size is practical for most trips

  • Spacious compartments help organized packing
  • Stylish modern design looks attractive

Cons

  • Premium luggage options offer smoother premium wheel systems
  • Dark colors may show dust and scuff marks more easily

Verdict

The American Tourister Ivy 2.0 Medium Trolley Bag is a reliable travel luggage option for Indian buyers looking for durability, smooth mobility, and practical storage without spending on luxury luggage brands.

Its biggest strengths are the durable polypropylene construction, smooth 360° spinner wheels, and balanced medium-size storage capacity that make it ideal for regular air travel, train journeys, business trips, and vacations. The lightweight handling and secure lock system also improve overall travel convenience.

While premium luxury luggage may offer even more refined wheel quality and advanced materials, for most travelers, this suitcase delivers excellent real-world usability and dependable value.

Best for:

  • Frequent travelers
  • Domestic and international trips
  • Business travel
  • Family vacations
  • Users wanting durable hard-shell luggage

Skip if:

  • You need extra-large family luggage
  • You prefer soft-shell expandable bags
  • You want ultra-premium luxury luggage features
