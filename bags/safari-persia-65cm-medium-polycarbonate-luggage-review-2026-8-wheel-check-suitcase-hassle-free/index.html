  • Medium 65cm Check-In Size – Ideal for 3–7 day trips, business travel, and family vacations.
  • Premium Polycarbonate Shell – Lightweight yet durable construction that offers better impact resistance than standard ABS luggage.
  • 8 Smooth-Rolling Spinner Wheels – Enables effortless 360-degree movement across airports, railway stations, and hotel lobbies.
  • Speed Wheel Technology – Designed for smoother maneuverability and reduced travel fatigue.
  • Spacious Interior Capacity – Accommodates clothing, footwear, accessories, and travel essentials efficiently.
  • Organized Packing Compartments – Helps keep belongings secure and neatly separated.
  • Durable Telescopic Trolley Handle – Provides comfortable handling and easy navigation.
  • Secure Locking System – Enhances safety for valuables and personal belongings during transit.
  • Scratch-Resistant Exterior Design – Helps maintain a premium appearance through regular travel use.
  • Lightweight Travel-Friendly Build – Easy to handle while maximizing storage space.

Pros

  • Polycarbonate shell offers excellent durability
  • Smooth 8-wheel spinner system improves mobility
  • Ideal size for medium-duration trips
  • Lightweight compared to many hard-shell alternatives
  • Attractive premium Pearl Blue finish
  • Organized interior packing layout
  • Easy maneuverability in crowded travel environments
  • Good balance between capacity and portability

Cons

  • Medium size may be insufficient for very long vacations
  • Premium polycarbonate luggage typically costs more than ABS alternatives
  • Hard-shell surfaces may develop cosmetic scratches over time
  • Not expandable in some variants
  • Fully loaded luggage can still be heavy to lift into overhead storage areas or vehicles

Verdict

The Safari Persia 65cm Polycarbonate Luggage is an excellent choice for travelers seeking a premium, lightweight, and durable check-in suitcase.

Its biggest strengths are the polycarbonate construction, smooth 8-wheel spinner system, and practical medium-sized capacity. These features make it particularly suitable for business travelers, frequent flyers, couples, and families taking short to medium-duration trips.

Compared to ABS luggage in the same category, the Persia offers better impact resistance, improved durability, and a more premium travel experience. For travelers looking for a reliable check-in suitcase that balances durability, mobility, and style, it is a strong contender.

Best for:

  • Business travelers
  • Domestic and international trips
  • 3–7 day vacations
  • Frequent flyers
  • Buyers seeking premium polycarbonate luggage

Skip if:

  • You need a very large suitcase for extended travel
  • You primarily take short weekend trips
  • You want expandable luggage with extra packing flexibility
