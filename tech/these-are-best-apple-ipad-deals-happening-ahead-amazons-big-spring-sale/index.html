These Are Best Apple iPad Deals Happening Ahead of Amazon’s Big Spring Sale

It's been six years since the legendary audio maker launched its first buds. I spent a week testing the latest iteration to see how they measure up.

By Karthik Published: Mar 24, 2026 2:42 PM IST

Board games are family staples, but lugging big boxes on the go isn’t very practical, especially if you’re traveling often. That’s where tiny games come in. Companies like World’s Smallest make miniature versions of some of the most popular games of all time—like Scrabble, Monopoly, and Connect 4—and they’re all pocket-sized, making them excellent travel companions for long flights or road trips. (And yes, they really work and aren’t just for show!)

mini card games

There are also mini card games (like Uno), solo games, and tiny retro arcade games (like Pac-Man and Tetris). And while the gameplay on some of these is abbreviated—or designed for fewer players—it's hard to resist reaching for them, even when you have the full-size originals on hand. Just be sure to note the age limits on each one and who you’re playing around, considering that most of these games contain tiny pieces that could be hazardous around small children.

We saved the best part for last: Most of them are $10 or less on Amazon, so they make fantastic stocking stuffers for the holidays, small birthday gifts, or little presents just because.
