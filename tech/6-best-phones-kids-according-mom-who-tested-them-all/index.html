The 6 Best Phones for Kids, According to a Mom Who Tested Them All

By Karthik Published: Mar 28, 2026 2:31 PM IST

After testing kid-specific phones and standard smartphones with parental controls, these are some of the best options.

The 6 Best Phones for Kids, According to a Mom Who Tested Them All

It’s hard to gauge just when each child is ready for their first phone. There’s a lot of factors that play into the decision—age, maturity, and each family’s individual needs. For us, living in a town where our kids walk to school propelled us to begin exploring kid-safe smart watches and cell phones earlier than our friends in the suburbs or rural areas. For other parents, after-school activities or shared custody situations mean a phone makes sense.

At whatever age parents make that decision, it’s important to choose a phone that provides some guardrails for safety while kids learn the ins and outs of phone ownership and etiquette. Dr. Devorah Heitner, author of Growing Up In Public, says there’s a reason this conversation can feel so stressful, but it doesn’t have to.

“Smartphones do more than most of us need; certainly more than a child or tween needs,” says Heitner. “Starting with a device that just offers voice calls and texting lets kids master those skills and gives them practice at independently communicating with friends or checking in with parents about after-school plans—without the distractions of games, YouTube or social media.” Eventually, teens can graduate to less locked-down devices once they’ve shown they’re ready.

Thankfully, there’s a robust list of choices on the market for kids, including traditional cellular phones, options that stay grounded at home, and smart watches that allow simple calls, texting, and GPS tracking. If you’re ready to get your kid an actual phone that can go right in their pocket or backpack, we’ve curated a list of some of the top options on the market to take the guesswork out of an otherwise fraught decision.

Best Phones for Kids

  • Best Overall: Samsung Galaxy A17
  • Best Budget: Pinwheel Genesis 4 Kids Smartphone
  • Best Parental Controls: Bark Cell Phone
  • Best for Teens: iPhone 17e
  • Best First Smartphone: Gabb Phone 4 Pro

How We Selected

As a mom of four, including three adolescents who’ve had cell phones for a bit now, we tested these phones in the real world. Beyond my personal experiences with my own kids, I leaned on my expertise as a tech writer who has been covering phones for kids since they first hit the market.

I’ve watched the operating systems go from clunky to smooth, and seen brands respond to what parents say they need in a phone for kids. I have interviewed hundreds of parents about their tech fears and questions, too. I get it, it’s a scary step.

Think of these phones as training wheels. Whether you’re looking for a simple flip phone or a smart phone with more options, there’s a choice that makes sense for your kid and your needs. You’re not handing your kid unfettered access to the internet. You’re lowering the risk of harm while giving them a safe way to learn about phone etiquette—and staying connected.
