Features

  • 1230mm Sweep Size – Suitable for medium to large bedrooms, halls, and living spaces.
  • BEE 2-Star Energy Efficient Rating – Helps reduce electricity consumption compared to conventional fans.
  • Aeroquiet Motor Technology – Designed for quieter and smoother operation.
  • Rust-Proof ABS Blades – Durable blades resistant to rust and easier to maintain.
  • High Air Delivery Performance – Provides strong airflow suitable for Indian summer conditions.
  • Modern Premium Design – Stylish appearance complements contemporary home interiors.
  • Lightweight ABS Construction – Reduces wobbling and improves durability.
  • Tangerine Brown Finish – Elegant color suitable for modern room aesthetics.
  • Reliable Motor Performance – Designed for long daily usage in Indian households.
  • 2-Year Warranty – Added reliability and service support from Orient Electric.

Pros

  • Quiet operation suitable for bedrooms
  • Good airflow performance for regular home usage
  • Energy-efficient compared to older ceiling fans
  • Rust-proof ABS blades improve durability
  • Modern design looks premium and stylish
  • Lightweight blades reduce vibration issues
  • Trusted Orient Electric brand support

Cons

  • 2-star rating is not as efficient as BLDC fans
  • Premium pricing compared to standard ceiling fans
  • Remote control not included in some variants
  • Airflow may feel moderate at lower speed settings
  • ABS blades may not appeal to buyers preferring metal blades

Verdict

The Orient Electric Aeroquiet Neu 1230mm Ceiling Fan is a solid choice for Indian buyers looking for a modern, stylish, and quieter ceiling fan for everyday home use.

Its biggest strengths are the silent motor performance, durable rust-proof ABS blades, and energy-efficient operation that make it suitable for bedrooms, living rooms, and offices. The modern design also gives it a more premium appearance compared to traditional ceiling fans.

While it may not deliver the extreme energy savings of BLDC fans, it still offers a good balance between airflow, design, comfort, and efficiency for regular household usage.

Best for:

  • Bedrooms and living rooms
  • Buyers wanting quieter ceiling fans
  • Modern home interiors
  • Daily household usage

Skip if:

  • You want maximum BLDC-level power savings
  • You prefer traditional metal-blade fans
  • You need smart remote-controlled fan features
